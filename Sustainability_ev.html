Our Commitment

Sustainability is embedded in every stage of our vehicles’ lifecycle – from design and sourcing to manufacturing and end‑of‑life recovery.

Key Initiatives

  • Use of recyclable and responsibly sourced materials in every cabin surface.
  • Energy‑efficient production supported by renewable power at core facilities.
  • Active battery recycling and second‑life programs to minimise waste.
  • Continuous aerodynamic and powertrain optimisation for lower lifetime emissions.
